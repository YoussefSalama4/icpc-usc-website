About
ICPC competition

The International Collegiate Programming Contest is an algorithmic programming contest for college students. Teams of three, representing their university, work to solve the most real-world problems, fostering collaboration, creativity, innovation, and the ability to perform under pressure. Through training and competition, teams challenge each other to raise the bar on the possible. Quite simply, it is the oldest, largest, and most prestigious programming contest in the world.
The contest fosters creativity, teamwork, and innovation in building new software programs, and enables students to test their ability to perform under pressure. The contest has raised aspirations and performance of generations of the world’s problem solvers in the computing sciences and engineering.
The ICPC features several levels of competition:
•Local Contests
•Regional Contests
•Regional Championships
•The World Finals



ICPC USC Community

A community of volunteers who wish to provide opportunity for and shine the spotlight on the students of computing.


Mission

To make ICPC training easier for students and providing them with information about topics related to it: programing, problem solving, Algorithms, data structures and competitive programing. We hope to benefit the world from our information and research and help people interested in computer science and competitive programing to know more about ICPC even how to participate on it

Vision

To make people and students interested in computer science and competitive programing because of our team, improve their skills and knowledge in programing and related science, facilitate their study of programing and training for problem solving through our insightful view. Moreover, we will connect programing lovers with the competitive programing for further benefit.

Values

To give everyone the power to create and share ideas and information without barriers.
• Work as a team to achieve our purposes.
• Become smarter and think differently.
• Exercise more on problem solving and competitive programming.
• Expand your programming vocabulary.
• Enhance your social life and creativity.
• Wide your social network.
